Introduction
Hi there! My name is Zach. I am 19 years old and will be 20 in March of 2022. I am a hobbyiest photographer, going to college to become a Child & Youth Worker. I've been pursuing to become a Child & Youth Worker professionally for about 2 years now. Helping people is my love and passion, I don't know what I would be doing without it. I've learned so much about the world and creativity through helping & caring for people, and I truly believe that it's a great way to express yourself as a individual.

Education
I am currently in my senior year of high school but i'm enrolled in college. I am learning all about mental health and troubled kids. In 2016-2017, I went to a troubled kids school because I needed professional help with coping with past traumatic issues I had when I was in the foster care system. There was a Child & Youth Worker there who inspired me to want to help kids who there parents have lost faith on and that still have hope of saving before they end up in prison. Before the pandemic, I frequently did photography, where I often captured nature & beauty.
